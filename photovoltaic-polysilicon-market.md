<p><ul><li>Granular</li><li>Blocky</li><li>Flaky</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The photovoltaic polysilicon market is categorized into four types based on particle morphology: granular, blocky, flaky, and powder. Granular polysilicon features larger, spherical particles that improve melting efficiency. Blocky polysilicon consists of larger, cubical shapes, offering enhanced purity. Flaky polysilicon has thin, flat particles that facilitate rapid dissolution, while powder polysilicon is finely milled for uniformity and versatility in solar cell production. Each type plays a crucial role in the efficiency and performance of photovoltaic systems.</p></p>
