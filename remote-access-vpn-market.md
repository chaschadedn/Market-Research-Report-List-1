<p><strong>The Remote-Access VPN Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SSL VPN</li><li>IPSec VPN</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Remote-Access VPN market is primarily categorized into SSL VPN and IPSec VPN. SSL VPNs utilize Secure Sockets Layer technology, offering simpler user access via web browsers, making them suitable for remote workers needing secure connectivity without extensive client software. In contrast, IPSec VPNs provide robust encryption and secure communication at the network layer, ideal for site-to-site connections and environments requiring high security. Both types cater to different organizational needs and user requirements for secure remote access.</p></p>
