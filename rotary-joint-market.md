<p><p>The rotary joint market comprises single circuit and double circuit types, essential for transferring fluids or gases in rotating systems. Single circuit rotary joints allow one medium to flow through, making them ideal for simpler applications. In contrast, double circuit rotary joints facilitate the simultaneous flow of two different media, enhancing efficiency in more complex systems. Both types serve critical roles in various industries, including manufacturing and aerospace, where uninterrupted operation and safe fluid transfer are vital.</p></p>
