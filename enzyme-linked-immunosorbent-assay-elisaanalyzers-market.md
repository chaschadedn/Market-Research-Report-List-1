<p><p>The Enzyme-linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA) analyzers market is segmented into two main types: Optical Filter ELISA Analyzers and Optical Grating ELISA Analyzers. Optical Filter ELISA Analyzers utilize specific wavelength filters to detect light emitted during the reaction, offering reliability and precision. Conversely, Optical Grating ELISA Analyzers use diffraction grating to analyze multiple wavelengths simultaneously, allowing for higher throughput and efficiency in high-volume testing. Both types are essential for accurate immunoassay measurements in various clinical and research applications.</p></p>
