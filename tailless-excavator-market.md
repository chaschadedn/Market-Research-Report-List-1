<p><p>The tailless excavator market comprises two primary types: crawler tailless excavators and wheeled tailless excavators. Crawler tailless excavators utilize tracks for mobility, offering superior stability and maneuverability on uneven terrains, making them ideal for construction and excavation projects. Wheeled tailless excavators, on the other hand, are designed for enhanced speed and maneuverability on paved surfaces, suitable for urban environments. Both types eliminate the need for a traditional tail, allowing for improved access in confined spaces.</p></p>
