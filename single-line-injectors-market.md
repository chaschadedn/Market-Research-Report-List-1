<p><p>Single-line injectors operate in two primary market types: parallel and progressive. In a parallel market, multiple injectors work simultaneously to deliver fuel, ensuring uniform distribution across all cylinders, thereby enhancing engine efficiency and performance. Conversely, the progressive market uses a sequential approach; injectors activate based on demand, optimizing fuel delivery according to engine needs. This can improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions. Both types aim to enhance vehicle performance while addressing different operational requirements in automotive applications.</p></p>
