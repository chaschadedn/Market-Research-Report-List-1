HomePlug utilizes existing electrical wiring for data transmission, facilitating networking without new wiring. Wireless M-Bus focuses on utility metering, providing efficient communication for energy and water consumption data. Other emerging technologies and protocols also contribute to the HAN landscape, enhancing interoperability and expanding smart home capabilities.</p></p>
