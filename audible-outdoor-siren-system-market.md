<p><p>The Audible Outdoor Siren System market is categorized into two main types: omni-directional and rotating directional sirens. Omni-directional sirens emit sound uniformly in all directions, ensuring broad coverage within a vicinity, making them ideal for alerting large areas. In contrast, rotating directional sirens focus sound in a specific direction, enhancing range and intensity in targeted zones, suitable for urban or complex environments. Both serve critical roles in emergency notifications and public safety, each with unique applications based on spatial requirements.</p></p>
