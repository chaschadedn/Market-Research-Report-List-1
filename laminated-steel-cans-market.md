<p><p>The laminated steel cans market comprises primarily two types: 2-piece cans and 3-piece cans. </p><p>2-piece cans are produced from a single sheet of metal, formed into a cylindrical shape with a lid, resulting in less material usage and a streamlined manufacturing process. In contrast, 3-piece cans consist of three parts: a cylindrical body and two ends, which are welded or soldered together. This type typically allows for greater versatility in design and size but may involve more complex production techniques. Both types cater to diverse packaging needs in food and beverage industries.</p></p>
