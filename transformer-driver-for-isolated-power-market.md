<p><p>The transformer driver for isolated power applications primarily features two market types: duty-cycle control and slew-rate control. Duty-cycle control adjusts the on-off timing of the transformer to efficiently manage power delivery, optimizing output based on load demands. In contrast, slew-rate control regulates the rate of voltage changes, minimizing electromagnetic interference and enhancing system stability. Both methods enhance the performance and reliability of isolated power systems, catering to various applications in telecommunications, industrial equipment, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
