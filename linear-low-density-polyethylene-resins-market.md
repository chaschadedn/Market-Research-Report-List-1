<p><p>Linear Low Density Polyethylene (LLDPE) resins are categorized into two main market types: Type I and Type II. Type I is primarily used in applications requiring higher strength and durability, such as stretch films and heavy-duty bags, due to its unique molecular structure that enhances tensile properties. Type II, on the other hand, is tailored for applications that prioritize flexibility and processability, making it ideal for products like flexible packaging and agricultural films. These distinctions cater to diverse industrial requirements and enhance performance across various uses.</p></p>
